SiniCAR

SiniCAR is a remake of the classic arcade game Sinistar. The player finds themselves driving in a parking lot and must collect fuel to keep moving and shopping carts to fire at The Biker, the boss enemy who chases the player around the map.


Technologies

  • Unity
  • C#

    • My Role

  • Game Developer
  • Game Designer
  • Unity Instructor
    • I was the only engineer on this team, and there was no dedicated game designer for this project, so I ended up "wearing many hats" for this project. I was the only person on the project who had used Unity or Source Control software, so I dedicated time to teaching the team's artists and producer what they needed to know.
