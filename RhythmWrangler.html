Rhythm Wrangler

My largest Unreal project to date, and my best project for gameplay development. Rhythm Wrangler is an arcade-style rhythm game where the user must throw their trusty lasso to the beat of the music to catch as many cows as possible. The player must simultaneously dodge UFOs trying to abduct both the player and their cows! The game integrates with the Twitch API to allow Twitch viewers to control the tempo of the music.
